OUR TRUSTED ORGANISATIONS
RSPCA (Royal Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals):
The RSPCA is a leading animal welfare charity in the United Kingdom. It operates rescue centers, hospitals, and clinics to provide care and support for animals in need. The organization also campaigns for stronger animal protection laws and promotes responsible pet ownership.
Animal Welfare Institute:
The Animal Welfare Institute (AWI) is a nonprofit organization that advocates for better treatment of animals through policy change, public awareness campaigns, and legal action. It focuses on issues such as animal testing, marine mammal protection, and wildlife conservation.
PETA (People for the Ethical Treatment of Animals):
PETA is a global animal rights organization that campaigns against animal cruelty and exploitation in all forms, including factory farming, animal testing, fur farming, and entertainment industries. It conducts investigations, rescues animals in distress, and advocates for legal and societal change to protect animals.
